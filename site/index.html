About

Cognisentia is the AI advisory practice of Jo Hunter, operating from Perth, Western Australia.

With deep engagement in AI since 2022, from board advisory and keynote speaking to hands-on capability building, Jo helps organisations confidently navigate the rapidly evolving AI landscape to become wise, responsible adopters of the technology.

Prior to focusing on AI, Jo spent over 15 years developing and implementing strategy across corporate, government, and not-for-profit sectors — from founding Pollen Strategy (clients including Tourism WA, 7-Eleven, and UWA) to senior strategic roles at HBF and within the WA health system.
